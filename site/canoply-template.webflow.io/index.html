Booking & Policies

Are there laundry facilities at the chalet?

Yes, we have a washer and dryer available for guest use, along with laundry detergent.

Is the chalet pet-friendly?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets to ensure the cleanliness and comfort of all our guests.

Do you provide linens and towels?

Yes, we provide fresh linens, bath towels, and extra blankets for your comfort.

What kitchen appliances and essentials are provided?

The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including a refrigerator, stove, oven, microwave, coffee maker, toaster, and dishwasher. We also provide cooking utensils, pots and pans, and basic spices.

Is there Wi-Fi available at the chalet?

Yes, we provide high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the chalet to keep you connected during your stay.

Are there any restaurants within walking distance?

Can you recommend any family-friendly attractions?

Absolutely! Families can enjoy the nearby adventure park with zip-lining and rock climbing, as well as scenic nature walks and a local wildlife sanctuary.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in begins at 3 PM, and check-out is by 11 AM. Early check-ins or late check-outs may be arranged upon request, depending on availability.

How do I check the availability and book a stay?

You can check availability and make a reservation through our 'Book Now' page. Simply select your dates, number of guests, and submit your booking request.

Do you require a security deposit?

Yes, we require a security deposit which will be fully refunded after your stay, provided there are no damages or policy violations.
